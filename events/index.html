Come Chat with Us at Zurich Life Science Day 2020

28.01.2020

Zurich Life Science Day is the largest career fair dedicated to Life Sciences in Switzerland. This anual event, organised by the Life Science Zurich Young Scientist Network, takes place at the Lichthof of the University of Zurich, Campus Irchel. Join us on the 28th of January 2020 and interact with representatives from more than 40 companies, in our company exhibition. Moreover, you have the chance to get your CV chacked and to take a professional CV picture!
