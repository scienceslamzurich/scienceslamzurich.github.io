Science Slam - The Abstract

Science Slam is the new form of scientific communication. The challenge lies in presenting one's research projects in an entertaining and easily understandable way - in no more than 10 minutes! Young scientists compete and try to impress the audience. All kinds of props and media are allowed and even encouraged. Many slammers will support their presentations with creative powerpoint slides, but anything goes. The audience decides who gets to the final round - and after that, who gets to be the next Science Slam Champion - by awarding them with the loudest applause of the night.

We accept and support all sciences - especially including the humanities.
