Zurich Life Science Day is the largest career fair dedicated to Life Sciences in Switzerland.

This anual event, organised by the Life Science Zurich Young Scientist Network, takes place at the Lichthof of the University of Zurich, Campus Irchel.

Join us on the 28th of January 2020 and interact with representatives from more than 40 companies, in our company exhibition. Moreover, you have the chance to get your CV chacked and to take a professional CV picture! Through out the day, 12 parallel sessions will help you clarify the day-to-day behind a job title. Last but not least, some companies will be providing 1:1 meetings: talk exclusively for 15 minutes with your selected company.

For more information, please refer to our website: www.lszysn.ch

Science Slam in a nutshell

Science Slam is the new form of scientific communication. The challenge lies in presenting one's research project in an entertaining and easily understandable way - in no more than 10 minutes! Young scientists compete and try to impress the audience. All kinds of props and media are allowed and even encouraged. Many slammers underline their statements with creative powerpoint slides while others rock the stage with their musical or poetic abilities. The audience decides who gets to the final round - and after that, who gets to be the next Science Slam Champion - by awarding them with the loudest applause of the night.

We accept and support all sciences - especially the humanities.
